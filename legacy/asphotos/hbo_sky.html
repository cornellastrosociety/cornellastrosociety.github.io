Mt. Pleasant Sky

When the members of CAS just can't take any more of Ithaca's light pollution, they pack up and head to nearby Mt. Pleasant. During one trip, Mike Roman took a few pictures of the skyline. Think you can recognize the constellations with so many stars? Try picking out the patterns, then mouse over the image to check.

Mike decided to edit one of the constellations a bit to make it look more like his dog. After all, he couldn't just let all those extra stars go to waste!
