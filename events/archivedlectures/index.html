Carl Sagan's 90th Birthday Information
The Cornell Astronomical Society is working with the Carl Sagan Institute to host a special lecture and viewing night, free and open to the public, in celebration of Professor Carl Sagan's 90th birthday.
Are We Alone? Searching for Life and Intelligence in the Universe
102 Toni Morrison Hall (Directions)
Prof. Shami Chatterjee
November 8th, 2024,
7 PM–8 PM
Toni Morrison Hall 102
People have wondered about life and intelligence beyond the Earth since the dawn of history. We will take a quick tour of searches for extraterrestrial intelligence (SETI), the quest for planets beyond our solar system, and the prospects for finding life and intelligence beyond the Earth with new telescopes and surveys.
Friday Open House at the Fuertes Observatory
November 8th, 2024,
8 PM–11 PM
Fuertes Observatory
Following the lecture by Professor Chatterjee in Toni Morrison Hall, the Fuertes Observatory will be open to the public for observing and tours of the historic telescope, artifacts, and facility. The Observatory will close one hour earlier than usual, at 11.
Special Saturday Open House at the Fuertes Observatory
November 9th, 2024,
10 PM–12 AM
Fuertes Observatory
Following a day of festivities hosted by the Carl Sagan Institute, the Fuertes Observatory will open to the public for a special Saturday open house from 10 PM until midnight, for observing and tours.
