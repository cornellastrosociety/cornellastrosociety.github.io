Some interesting links...

CU Astronomy Cornell's Department of Astronomy

General Astronomy Information

Amazing Space A website run by the Space Telescope Science Institute. Geared mainly towards children and teachers, though there's something for everyone.
Ask an Astronomer A huge collection of astronomy related questions, answered by the astronomers at Cornell.
Astronomy Picture of the Day Discover the cosmos! Each day features a new astronomical image with an informative description.
Bad Astronomy Debunks common misconceptions in astronomy.
SkyTonight Formerly Sky & Telescope.com, this website is a good source for accurate astronomy news. It also contains everything from a customizable star chart to tips for buying your first telescope to articles on advanced image processing.

For Observers

ClearDarkSky Forecaster of astronomical observing conditions for over 700 observatories and observing sites in North America. Check out the Ithaca page.
Cloudy Nights Read reviews of amateur astronomy equipment.
Cornell Weather Page Weather forecast for Ithaca
Heavens-above.com Get predictions of satellite passes here. The International Space Station, Iridiums, HST etc. can be seen with the naked eye. Predictions for Ithaca here .
Note: some people have found that Internet Explorer crashes if they try to visit this site while blocking cookies.
SETI@home Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence at home.
Solar Terrestrial Dispatch Get advance notice of possible aurora activity.

Astronomy Organizations

American Association of Variable Star Observers Amateur astronomers who want to contribute to research may want to check out this site.
International Comet Quarterly Ephemerides, orbital data, and news of comets.
International Dark-Sky Association "To preserve and protect the nighttime environment and our heritage of dark skies through quality outdoor lighting."
International Meteor Organization Find out how amateur astronomers can contribute to science. Contains plenty of information on meteor showers.
