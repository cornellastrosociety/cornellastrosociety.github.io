Moon Dog

Mike Roman took this picture of a "moon dog" on the evening of March 30, 2007. The moon itself is on the right, partly obscured by altocumulus clouds. The reddish ring around the moon is caused by light being scattered through the clouds. The moon dog is on the left side of the image. It's a bit faint, but you should be able to see the characteristic red-yellow-blue color pattern.
